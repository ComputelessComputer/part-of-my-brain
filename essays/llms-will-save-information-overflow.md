The world is flooding with contents of all sorts. People don't stop consuming them. Even when they're eating, they'd watch videos on YouTube. The real issue with contents though, is that they're still localized, which also differs on a scale. Also, they're hard to be organized. It's hard for people nowadays to organize and structure information into a conclusion, since they get bombarded with them. So it all comes down to a needs for these roles, and LLMs are a perfect utility for this purpose.
