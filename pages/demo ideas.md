## neal.fun / cursor camp
- url: https://neal.fun/cursor-camp/
- date stashed: 2026-04-30
- format: scrollytelling where your cursor becomes the protagonist — you guide a tiny character through a "camp" experience by moving the mouse
- why it's cool: turns the most boring UI primitive (the cursor) into the demo. zero friction onboarding, instant interactivity, memorable.
- char angle: imagine a landing page where the cursor *is* the meeting attendee. you scroll, the cursor "takes notes," and by the end you've seen char in action without watching a single video. or: cursor-as-agent walking through a transcript live.
